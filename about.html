About AUIS Voice

History

AUIS Voice is the first independent English Student Newspaper in Iraq, published at the American University of Iraq, Sulaimani.

The Voice is an independent publication and is not connected to any political party, religious group or ethnic group. The Voice is an editorial that appears without a byline and represents the views of the student editorial board of the paper.

In 2010, some active and passionate students came up to have a newspaper such that they could express their opinions and ideas, and finally they announced AUIS Voice.

The voice is still available, and it is somehow active in such a way that students can publish their article and opinions regarding anything that interests them and related to the context of AUIS.
